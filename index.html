About

I am currently a Postdoctoral Research Scientist advised by Prof. Junfeng Yang at Columbia University. I am generally interested in security and privacy problems, including software security, web security, and language-based security.

I earned my Ph.D. from the Chinese University of Hong Kong advised by Prof. Wei Meng. I received my B.Eng. from University of Chinese Academy of Sciences.
