About Me

I am a third-year Ph.D. candidate at Department of Computer Science and Engineering, The Chinese University of Hong Kong. I am very fortunate to work with my advisor Prof. Wei Meng and other members in SecLab here.

Currently, I am also a visiting student in Tsinghua University advised by Prof. Chao Zhang.

I received my bachelor's degree from University of Chinese Academy of Sciences in 2019, where I had fruitful research experience with Prof. Kai Chen working on my thesis project. I was born in Changsha, Hunan Province, China.

I am on the job market for 2023 fall intake. If you have any openings related to software security, I would love to get in contact!

Research Interests

  • Software security and program analysis.

  • Fuzz testing, symbolic execution, and their co-design.
